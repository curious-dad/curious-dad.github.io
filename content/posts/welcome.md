## Start with a question

The best family conversations often begin with a simple question: *Why?*

This blog is a place to follow those questions a little further. I’ll be collecting lessons from parenting, notes from projects, and small observations that are too useful—or too delightful—to leave in a notebook.

Some posts will be polished. Some will be field notes. All of them will start with curiosity.

Thanks for being here.
